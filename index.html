Why We Exist

It started with one Debbie.

Ever since I was a boy, I remember my mum visiting people who needed a friend. Not because anyone asked her to. Just because she noticed — a neighbour who seemed quiet, someone sitting alone, a person who clearly needed someone to show up.

And she’d go. Regularly. Reliably. Warmly. People noticed. Over the years, organisations began asking her to do it properly — to come alongside people who were lonely, isolated, ageing without company. She said yes. She gave herself to it completely.

Watching her do that — for years, for all kinds of people — I kept thinking the same thing: everyone deserves a friend like Debbie. Everyone needs someone who’ll simply show up.

So I built a way to find one.

Every companion we recruit is held to her standard — warm, patient, present, genuinely caring. If I wouldn’t trust them with my own mum, they don’t work for us.

— Josh, Founder

Rooted in something deeper.

We started this because we believe something simple: every person, regardless of age, background, or belief, deserves to feel genuinely seen and loved.

Our values are rooted in our Christian faith — dignity, presence, love, service. You'll feel that in the way we work, the people we choose, and the care we take with every match.

Some clients enjoy a quiet prayer at the end of a visit. It's always offered, never assumed. Our companions come from all walks of life, and our doors are open to everyone.

Everyone is welcome here. Always.
